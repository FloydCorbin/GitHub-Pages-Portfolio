About

Floyd is a Lead Quality Engineer at Rightpoint. As a quality assurance engineer, Floyd seeks to promote best practices to help drive quality internally on his projects. He seeks to be involved in project discussions and planning earlier, to promote a shift left QA mentality. He reviews and validates functional requirements against non-functional requirements to identify potential conflicts or risks to the project delivery timeline.

Floyd's expertise is in overall project development. He has worked on AEMaaCS Sites, and Assets projects while with Rightpoint. Recent projects have been for companies such as Oneblood.org, Grand Design RV, Pluralsight, and Bungie Interctive.

Before Rightpoint, Floyd worked for MRM/McCann on the PHRMA.org Sitecore 9 Program of projects, including one primary site with a few micro-sites. Floyd also worked on a project for the Panasonic Configurator, an application to configure the Toughbook line of computers, which included intensive business logic testing. Before MRM/McCann, Floyd worked as a Project Manager/Business Analyst for the State of Utah on several projects for the Prescription Drug Monitoring Program (PMP) Interconnect application, including a project to connect Utah's PMP to all other US States.
