About

Floyd holds the position of Lead Quality Engineer at Rightpoint. As a quality assurance engineer, Floyd strives to advocate for best practices, fostering internal quality on his projects. He actively engages in project discussions and early-stage planning, aiming to instill a 'shift-left' QA mentality. Floyd meticulously reviews and validates functional requirements against non-functional ones, identifying potential conflicts or risks to the project delivery timeline.

Floyd's expertise spans overall project development, including work on AEMaaCS Sites and Assets projects during his tenure at Rightpoint. Noteworthy recent projects include collaborations with companies such as Oneblood.org, Grand Design RV, Pluralsight, and Bungie Interactive.

Prior to joining Rightpoint, Floyd contributed his skills at MRM/McCann, where he played a vital role in the PHRMA.org Sitecore 9 Program. This involved managing multiple projects, including a primary site with several micro-sites. Floyd also contributed to a project for the Panasonic Configurator, an application designed to configure the Toughbook line of computers, involving intensive business logic testing.

Before his time at MRM/McCann, Floyd served as a Project Manager/Business Analyst for the State of Utah, working on various projects for the Prescription Drug Monitoring Program (PMP) Interconnect application. His responsibilities included leading a project to connect Utah's PMP to all other U.S. states.
